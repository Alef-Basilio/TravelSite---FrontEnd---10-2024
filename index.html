Val di Funes is situated in the northeastern part of Italy, near the Austrian border. It's nestled in the Dolomite mountains, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Val di Funes is known for its breathtaking natural beauty, with picturesque villages, rolling hills, and towering mountains. The valley is surrounded by the Odle and Geisler mountain groups.
